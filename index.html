What I Do

I create immersive audio experiences for video games. From atmospheric soundscapes to dynamic sound effects, I design audio that enhances storytelling and engages players deeply. My work covers a range of genres, ensuring each project’s unique world has its own voice, whether through haunting melodies, adrenaline-pumping tracks, or subtle ambient sounds. I’m passionate about blending sound and music to shape unforgettable game moments.

Who I Am

I’m Viktor Kraus, a dedicated video game composer and sound designer. My journey in game audio started from a love for storytelling and interactive worlds, and I’ve since worked with developers to bring their games to life sonically. I believe sound is the unseen dimension of games, one that can evoke emotions and create memories. Outside of audio work, I enjoy exploring new music styles and discovering unique sounds that inspire my creative process.
